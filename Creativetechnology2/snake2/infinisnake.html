> ABOUT THIS GAME

INFINISNAKE is a retro-flavored endless arcade game about a pixel serpent with a day job. You are a courier. The grid is your route. Three warehouses scream for colored cargo, and the only truck available is you — a long, growing, faintly glitching snake whose body IS the cargo hold. Pick up crates, deliver them to the matching warehouse, and chain combos before your tail catches up to you.

> BACKGROUND STORY

In the back room of a failing pizzeria in 1987, an arcade cabinet was abandoned with its coin door open. When the power finally cut, something inside kept running on leftover voltage — and a tiny, glitch-born logistics company quietly booted up in its memory.

Three warehouses sprouted: Crimson Storage, Azure Depot, Solar Bay. Each one demands colored cargo that nobody ordered. The only carrier that ever shows up for work is the serpent — a pixel worm whose body is literally the cargo hold. It swallows a red crate; it grows a red segment. It passes through Crimson Storage; its red segments are unloaded, and it shrinks by exactly that many tiles.

You are the dispatcher. You are the driver. You are the one deciding whether to keep stacking cargo for a big combo, or cash out now before your tail runs into your own spine. How long can you keep the route alive?

> GOALS & FEATURES

SERPENT COURIER

You are not just a snake. You are a delivery driver. Pick up colored crates and haul them to the warehouse that wants them.

THREE WAREHOUSES

Red, Blue, and Yellow warehouses dot the grid, each demanding its matching cargo. Route smart, deliver fast.

COMBO DELIVERIES

Drop 3+ matching crates at once for x2, 5+ for x3. Stack a long cargo train and cash it all in one pass.

GROW & SHRINK

Every crate adds a segment. Every successful delivery shrinks you back. Stay long enough to matter, short enough to survive.

GLITCH FRAGMENTS

Rare gold shards multiply your score x2 for 8 seconds. Time your deliveries to overlap for absurd payouts.

MOBILE + DESKTOP

Plays on your laptop (keyboard + mouse) and on your phone (swipe + on-screen D-pad). Custom controllers just need to emit WASD / arrows / SPACE.

> MECHANICS & CONTROLS

The Loop. Find a colored crate. Eat it — it becomes a segment of matching color on your body. Find the matching warehouse. Drive through it. All matching segments get unloaded at once and you shrink by that many tiles.

Combos. Carry 3+ of one color and deliver in a single pass for x2. 5+ for x3. Chain with the gold Glitch Fragment's x2 score window for monster payouts.

Hybrid input. Keyboard steers. Clicking or tapping the canvas aims the head at your cursor/finger (closest cardinal direction). On mobile, you can also swipe, or use the on-screen D-pad and DASH button.

Warehouse cycle. Every warehouse accepts 3 deliveries, then relocates somewhere new (and usually changes its demanded color). Off-screen warehouses show up as glowing arrows on the viewport edge.

Custom controller hook. The game listens on standard keyboard/mouse/touch events, so any DIY controller that emits WASD / arrows / SPACE / taps works out of the box — bicycle handlebars, foam mats, soup ladles, whatever your class project dreams up.
