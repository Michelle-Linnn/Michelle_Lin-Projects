Project Summary

Aura Cookie Machine

This project is designed to help individuals understand and enhance their personal aura, boosting confidence and influence. We conducted research from various aspects, including attire, body language, eye contact, and professional habits.

Based on our findings, we have developed a unique cookie machine, which allows individuals to "eat" their own aura cookies to enhance the strength of their aura. This interactive approach encourages personal growth and self-awareness.
