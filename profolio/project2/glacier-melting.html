Project 02 Data Visualization / Sustainable Material Design

Melting Pulse

Translating the silent disappearance of glaciers into tangible data and bio-material installations.

My Role

Data Artist, Material Researcher

Focus

Eco-friendly Materials, Data Storytelling

Project Summary

Vanishing Archives

This project explores the intersection of climate data and material science. By visualizing the melting rate of Arctic ice, I created an installation that uses sustainable materials to mimic the fragile state of our glaciers.

Step 01

Data Visualization

The visualization converts global temperature anomalies and sea-level rise data into a fluid, disappearing digital landscape.

Step 02

Materiality

I experimented with various eco-friendly materials, including Bioplastics and recycled polymers, to achieve the crystalline yet fleeting texture of ice.

Step 03

The Installation

A physical manifestation of the data, using the developed bio-materials to represent the melting pulse of the earth.
