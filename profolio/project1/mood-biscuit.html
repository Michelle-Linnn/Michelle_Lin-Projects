Project 01 User Experience Design / Interactive Design

Mood biscuit

Studied the changes in aura for different individuals. People can enhance the strength of their aura by "eating" their own aura cookies.

My Role

UX Researcher, 3D Modeler, Interaction Designer

Tools Used

Rhino, Keyshot, Figma, Arduino, p5.js

Project Summary

Aura Cookie Machine

This project is designed to help individuals understand and enhance their personal aura, boosting confidence and influence. We conducted research from various aspects, including attire, body language, eye contact, and professional habits.

Step 01

Preliminary Research

At the initial stage of the project, we deconstructed the abstract concept of "Aura" from multiple dimensions. By observing the daily behaviors of people from different professional backgrounds, we established the initial connection between visual feedback and psychological suggestion.

Step 02

Influencing Factors

We delved into the core elements affecting personal aura, including attire, body language, eye contact, and professional habits.

Step 03

Space Theory

A study on spatial theory. We explored how the physical placement of the interaction machine affects users' emotional perception.

01. Ideation

Aura Categorization

I found that "aura" can be divided into two types: internal and external: inner aura can be personality, which is the accumulation of life experiences and is difficult to change.. However, the external aura can be changed by clothing, body language, eyes and occupation type, and can be adjusted subjectively.

02. Concept

Concept flow chart of biscuit machine

By eating biscuits with shapes that represent each person’s unique aura, you can convey a hint of encouragement and recognition, and use your behavior to influence psychology.

Such behavior can inspire individuals to recognize and accept their own uniqueness and promote a confident transformation of their aura.. At the same time, this can also convey a message of respect and tolerance to others, encourage them to accept the differences of others, and establish positive interactions in getting along with each other.
