Pulse of Decay

MULTIMEDIA INSTALLATION // DIGITAL CORE // MICHELLE LIN
DATA SPEAKS: Every falling particle represents an actual monthly loss in Greenland's ice mass, now collapsing at -265.80 Gt/yr. This screen isn't just data; it is the fading pulse of our planet's primary regulator. As you view this through my installation of reclaimed materials, consider how our legacy can be one of restoration, not disintegration.
