Introduction
This is a private emotional journaling system designed specifically for individuals. By translating fleeting, vague emotions into concrete geometric language, it facilitates the entire process from "feeling" to "identifying" to "archiving."
01 The Core Ideogram — Sensibility
Position: Front of the card.
Logic: Drawn according to "Vertical Axis + Geometric Core + Directional Accents".
Function: The raw portrait of an emotion. The Axis represents "Self", the Core represents "Mental State" (Circle: Spirit, Square: Stagnation, Diamond: Conflict), and Accents represent "Energy Flow".
02 The Shorthand Texture — Efficiency
Position: Back of the card (Visual Center).
Logic: Macro-compression of the front. It strips fine lines, retaining the core skeleton with injected stripe textures.
Function: "Instant Recognition" system. Users recall the emotional baseline through visual intuition without reading text.
03 The Cipher Archive — Rationality
Position: Back of the card (Surrounding Shorthand).
Logic: A-Z Encryption system. Classifies letters into varied geometric forms.
Function: Narrative archive. It geometrically reconstructs private thoughts, maintaining visual unity while storing precise language.

Geometric Gene Sharing
A. Genetic Inheritance: The Geometric Core of the front-side symbol determines the primary form of the back-side Shorthand while predetermining the Cipher's visual distribution.

B. The Collapse of Meaning: This system demonstrates information's evolution from infinite sensibility (front) to semi-structuralization (shorthand), culminating in full structuralization (cipher). This connection ensures consistency with the original emotional logic.
