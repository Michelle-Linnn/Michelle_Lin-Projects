ABOUT

I'm Wenjie Lin (Michelle), a multidisciplinary designer based in Pasadena, CA — currently pursuing an MFA in Media Design Practices at ArtCenter College of Design.


My work lives at the edge of physical and digital — interaction design, creative coding, and material systems that ask people to feel something, not just use something. From biscuit machines that visualize your aura, to climate installations that make data tangible, I design experiences with precision and curiosity.


I also founded Sweetpaper, an independent design studio, where I design GWP packaging and brand identities for clients — including work for Gree-bo, a pet blind box brand.


I'm drawn to BMW Group's commitment to precision, human-centered thinking, and the future of mobility. I want to bring my cross-disciplinary perspective to that work.
